Whether you are an expert 3D engineer/designer or just a hobbyist who is only entering the world of 3D, you will enjoy working with SelfCAD. Why spending months learning how work with expansive and complicated software? In SelfCAD everything is so simple that you can create great things within few minutes. SelfCAD allows you to do magic to your shapes and make all of them 3D printable. So if you decide to get a 3D printer or already have one, SelfCAD will be perfect for you.
